My Top 10 Anime List

Welcome to my blog where I share the top 10 animes according to me!

1. One Piece

One Piece follows Monkey D. Luffy, a boy whose body gains the properties of rubber after eating a Devil Fruit. Along with his diverse crew of pirates, known as the Straw Hat Pirates, Luffy explores the Grand Line, in search of the world's ultimate treasure known as "One Piece" to become the next Pirate King.

2. Attack on Titan

Set in a world where humanity is on the brink of extinction due to giant humanoid creatures called Titans, Eren Yeager joins the military to fight back and uncover the truth behind the Titans' origins, alongside his friends Mikasa and Armin.

3. Naruto

Naruto Uzumaki, a young ninja with dreams of becoming the strongest ninja and the Hokage of his village, faces many challenges growing up as the host of the Nine-Tailed Fox demon. Alongside his friends, Naruto battles enemies and strives to prove his worth to the world.

4. Demon Slayer

Set in Taisho-era Japan, Demon Slayer follows Tanjiro Kamado, a kind-hearted boy who becomes a demon slayer after his family is slaughtered and his sister, Nezuko, is turned into a demon. He embarks on a journey to save his sister and hunt down demons.

5. My Hero Academia

In a world where almost everyone has superpowers called "Quirks," young Izuku Midoriya, who was born without a quirk, dreams of becoming the greatest hero like his idol, All Might. After a fateful encounter, he inherits All Might's power and starts his journey at U.A. High School to become a professional hero.

6. Bleach

Ichigo Kurosaki, a high school student with the ability to see ghosts, becomes a Soul Reaper after accidentally absorbing the powers of another Reaper, Rukia Kuchiki. Ichigo takes on the responsibilities of protecting humans from evil spirits while battling Hollows and other supernatural threats.

7. Jujutsu Kaisen

Yuji Itadori, a high school student, inadvertently becomes the host for a powerful cursed spirit known as Ryomen Sukuna. To save others from the curse, Yuji joins the Jujutsu Sorcerers to exorcise curses and control the evil spirit within him.

8. Hunter x Hunter

Hunter x Hunter follows Gon Freecss, a young boy who discovers that his father, who left him when he was young, is a world-renowned "Hunter," a licensed profession for those who are excellent in a range of dangerous tasks. Gon sets out on a journey to become a Hunter and find his father.

9. Fullmetal Alchemist: Brotherhood

In a world governed by the law of alchemy, brothers Edward and Alphonse Elric search for the Philosopher's Stone to restore their bodies after a failed alchemy experiment leaves them damaged. Along the way, they uncover dark secrets of their government and the truth behind alchemy.

10. Fairy Tail

Fairy Tail follows the adventures of Natsu Dragneel, a dragon slayer wizard, and his friends from the Fairy Tail guild. Together, they take on dangerous missions, face powerful enemies, and form unbreakable bonds in the magical land of Fiore.
